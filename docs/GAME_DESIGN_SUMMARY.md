## THE PHILOSOPHY PLAYBOOK SYSTEM

### The Core Innovation
DJ Jones creates a translation system where:
- **Formations** = Philosophical positions
- **Plays** = Arguments
- **Route trees** = Lines of reasoning
- **Coverage reads** = Objections and responses
- **Audibles** = Alternative interpretations
- **Protection schemes** = Defenses against counter-arguments

### Real Philosophy Curriculum
Based on actual PhD comprehensive reading lists:

**Ancient (The "Classical Formation")**:
- Plato: Republic, Phaedo, Symposium, Meno
- Aristotle: Nicomachean Ethics, Metaphysics, De Anima

**Modern (17th-18th Century)**:
- Descartes: Meditations on First Philosophy
- Locke: Essay Concerning Human Understanding
- Leibniz: Monadology
- Berkeley: Principles
- Hume: Treatise, Enquiry

**Critical Turn**:
- Kant: Critique of Pure Reason, Groundwork

**19th-20th Century**:
- Mill: Utilitarianism, On Liberty
- Nietzsche: Genealogy of Morals
- Husserl: Ideas
- Heidegger: Being and Time
- Wittgenstein: Tractatus, Philosophical Investigations
- Sartre: Being and Nothingness

### Playbook Notation Examples

```
PLT-CV-I = Plato-Cave-Initial
DESC-COG-I = Descartes-Cogito-Initial
KANT-CI-I = Kant-Categorical-Imperative-Initial
MILL-UTIL-A = Mill-Utilitarianism-Act
MILL-UTIL-R = Mill-Utilitarianism-Rule
NIETZ-GM-I = Nietzsche-Genealogy-Master
SART-BF = Sartre-Bad-Faith
```

### The Big Book
A massive three-ring binder created during the all-nighter:
- Section 1: Ancient Offense (Plato, Aristotle)
- Section 2: Modern Spread (Descartes, Hume, Kant)
- Section 3: Contemporary Defense (Mill, Nietzsche, Sartre)
- Section 4: Special Teams (Logic, Ethics, Metaphysics)
- Section 5: Game Day Call Sheet (Thesis defense prep)
